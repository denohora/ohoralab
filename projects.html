    • Price your Vice

    Price your Vice is part of the RISK LAB, running in The Science Gallery, an entertaining and educational interactive museum of science. RISK LAB explores the psychology and mathematics that underlie risks, our ability to evaluate those risks and make appropriate choices. The Price your Vice project measures delay discounting, a characteristic of human decision making involved in health and financial risks.

    Collaborators: Dr. Maciej Dabrowski , Rachel Carey, Aoife Kervick, David Crowley
