Welcome!

Thank you for agreeing to participate in this experiment.

There are three parts. First, you will complete a set of questionnaires based on information provided to you. You can begin this by pressing the Time 1 button.

Then you will participate in a short distraction task. Click the Distraction Button when the experimenter asks you to begin this stage

Finally, we will ask you to fill out the questionnaires a second time. Click the appropriate Time 2 button when the experimenter asks you to do so.


Please feel free to ask quesions prior to the start of the study. If you feel that you need to leave at any time, please let the experimenter know. Thank you very much for your time and enthusiasm.
